I am a founder and blockchain engineer working in crypto since 2017. Currently, I am working as an independent researcher and collaborating on open-source software in the Ethereum Ecosystem. I'm focusing on DeFi and other financial innovations that will shape the future of money.

I believe strongly in the tenants of decentralization and open-source software. We cannot take shortcuts. We must build a parallel financial system that is open and accessible to all, and out of control from centralized authorities.

Currently, I'm looking to collaborate with other engineers and researchers on open-source projects. If you are interested in collaborating, please reach out.

Why Ethereum and DeFi?

Ethereum because it sticks true to the ethos of decentralization better than any other blockchain.

I'm drawn to DeFi, seeing it as blockchain's pivotal innovation poised to challenge both commercial and central banks. While the internet reshaped most sectors, banking remained untouched due to strict regulations. Now, with central banks printing vast sums and the rise of CBDCs capable of monitoring our spending, we tread on unsettling ground.

I also am excited in other financial innovations that don't fall under DeFi. I believe there will be many categories of financial innovation with blockchains, such as DeFi, OpenFi, and CoFi, which I have explained in a recent blog.
